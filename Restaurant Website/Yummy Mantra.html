Home of Happiness

Yummy Mantra celebrates recipes handed down from generation to generation by Italian immigrants. Our dishes are served family-style, meant to be shared by the entire table. Every Yummy Mantra offers an eclectic setting designed to make every occasion a party.

Experience

Your experience won't be complete without having a meal at some of the best fine dining restaurants in the Philippines at Yummy Mantra. We have a number of restaurants that offer delectable cuisines from around the world for those who are looking to satisfy their cravings.

Guest

Guests can also have their fill at casual dining restaurants that offer international dishes to a more diverse palate. For Latin-cuisine, Waterside Restaurant is a must-try. We have everything covered- from Italian specialties to Korean delicacies, experience fun casual feasts and fine dining at its best.

Indulge

Meat eaters who love to indulge in premium steaks with an Italian flair can dine at Finestra Italian Steakhouse. Choose between Australian Blackmore and US Snake River Farms Wagyu beef cuts, and... have it grilled to perfection by Italian Chef Alan Marchetti.
